Welcome to Kivulini

Experience the Ultimate Beachfront Getaway

At Kivulini Beach Villas, the azure waters and pristine sands of Diani create the perfect backdrop for your dream vacation. Our luxurious accommodations, exceptional dining, and array of activities ensure a memorable stay for every guest.

Choose Room

Beautiful Villas by the coast

Nestled along the pristine shores of Diani Beach, Kivulini Beach Villas offers an enchanting escape in an elegantly designed Arab-style home. Spacious villas, adorned with traditional Lamu furniture and vibrant coastal fabrics, provides breathtaking views of the sea and lush coconut groves, ensuring a tranquil and luxurious stay.

Calming Pools

Unwind in the refreshing pool, surrounded by swaying palm trees and a serene tropical ambiance. Whether taking a relaxing dip or soaking up the sun on the poolside loungers, the peaceful setting of Kivulini Beach Villas ensures ultimate relaxation, making it a perfect retreat for couples, families, and friends.

Private Beach Access

Step directly onto the soft, white sands of Diani Beach from your villa. Kivulini Beach villas offers exclusive access to a secluded beach stretch, perfect for sunbathing, evening strolls, or simply unwinding with the soothing sound of ocean waves.
